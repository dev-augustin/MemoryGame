Game Rules:
  • All tiles face down. Turn over any two tiles
  • If the two tiles match, you win. Else tiles flip over to face down
  • Remember what was on each tile and where it was
  • Watch and remember during the other player's turn
  • The game is over when all the tiles have been matched
  • The player with the most matches wins
